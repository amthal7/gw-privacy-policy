Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08/03/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to GW Sales Rep ("App"). This App is designed for internal use by authorized sales representatives of our company to manage POS terminal visits. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data and device permissions within the App.

2. Information We Collect

GW Sales Rep does not collect, store, or share any personal data. However, the App may require access to certain device features to perform its intended functions.

3. Camera Permissions & Usage

The App requests access to your device's camera for the following purposes:

  • Photo Capture: To take a photo of the POS terminal location as proof of visit.
  • QR Code Scanning: To scan QR codes and mark sales visits for internal tracking.

Important: The App does not store these images or QR code data permanently on your device or any external servers. All data usage is strictly for internal verification.

4. Data Security

GW Sales Rep does not transmit any personal information over the internet. All operations are conducted locally within the App to ensure maximum security and privacy.

5. Third-Party Access

The App does not share any data with third parties. It is strictly used for internal operations by our company’s sales representatives.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the App or on our official company website.
